About Me

Shariq Khan is a third year student in I.T.S Engineering College pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science with a passion of being an enthusiast coder and making his name in the Software and IT industry. He comes from Lucknow with a good educational background and is working hard towards his goals. In his free time, he loves to play music and sing along.
The idea of this website comes from his second love, Music. He had a hard time learning to play guitar because of the less resources he had. Searching online for the videos, lessons and guidance takes a lot of your time when you are trying to learn something new. So, in this website he has organized everything required for a beginner to learn a guitar. From buying one to the required videos and YouTube links.
Hope you guys will have a great time learning music.
